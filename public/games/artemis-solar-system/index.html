Artemis reference

NASA’s current Artemis III page describes a 2027 crewed demonstration in low Earth orbit, not a confirmed lunar landing. This prototype therefore uses an Artemis-style Orion/SLS lunar reference: Moon mean distance ÷ configurable outbound transit days. It is a gameplay estimate, not NASA flight dynamics.

Model limits

Planet positions use JPL approximate Keplerian elements for the eight planets where available. Orbit drawing is 2D and compressed so the outer planets fit on screen. The route line is a simplified instantaneous chord, not a powered gravity-assist mission design.

  • Planet diameters/distances: NASA/JPL Solar System Sizes and Distances.
  • Mean orbital velocities: NASA Basics of Space Flight, Solar System chapter.
  • Planet positions: JPL Solar System Dynamics approximate planetary elements.
  • Moon, Pluto and Ceres facts: NASA Science.
